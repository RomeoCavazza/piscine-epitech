# Seminar: Organizational Theory & Strategy

Mastering the mechanisms of professional structures: from traditional hierarchies to agile startups. Understanding how information flows, decisions are made, and operational efficiency is organized and optimized.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Core Objectives**: 
> - **Organizational Audit**: Analyzing real-world companies (Conservatoire, Epitech, Agence).
> - **Comparative Analysis**: Identifying strengths, weaknesses, and optimization opportunities.
> - **Strategic Design**: Creating a target organizational blueprint.
> - **Expansion Models**: Planning for remote/distributed operations.
