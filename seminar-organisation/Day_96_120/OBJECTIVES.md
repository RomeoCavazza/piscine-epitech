# Days 96–120 — Objectives & Learning Outcomes

## Core Objectives

- **Organizational Auditing**: Systematically analyzing real companies' structures and processes
- **Comparative Analysis**: Cross-org pattern recognition and benchmarking
- **Strategic Design**: Creating target organizational models based on theory + practice
- **Remote Expansion**: Planning scalable distributed organizational models
- **Professional Communication**: Presenting complex analysis to stakeholders

## Key Concepts

### Organizational Structures
- Functional hierarchies and departmental organization
- Matrix and flat organizational models
- Centralized vs. distributed decision-making

### Analysis Methods
- SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)
- Process mapping and workflow visualization
- Stakeholder interviews and survey design
- Comparative benchmarking

### Strategic Models
- Mintzberg's organizational configurations
- Lewin's change management model
- Crozier & Friedberg's organizational analysis
- Agile vs. traditional organizational paradigms

## Learning Outcomes

By completion, students will:
1. **Audit** organizational structures with structured methodology
2. **Identify** key dysfunctions and single points of failure
3. **Propose** actionable optimization solutions
4. **Design** scalable organizational models
5. **Plan** distributed/remote workforce operations
6. **Present** complex organizational strategy to leadership
